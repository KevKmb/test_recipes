Guacamole

Description

My favorite easy to make dip for snacks

Ingredients

  • 2-3 ripe avocados
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 100g of cherry tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons of vegan créme fraiche
  • 1/2 of a lime, juice squeezed

Steps

  1. Slice the avocados open and remove the core. Scoop the pulp out of the avocados, put it in a bowl
    and mash them with a fork
  2. Peel the garlic and use a garlic press to mince it. Add it into the bowl
  3. Finely chop the tomatoes and add them
  4. Finally add the créme fraiche & lime juice and stir it all together
